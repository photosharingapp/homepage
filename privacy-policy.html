Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, upload photos, or contact us for support. This includes:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Photos and media files you upload
  • Payment information (processed securely through Razorpay)
  • Usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement military-grade encryption to protect your data. Your photos and personal information are stored securely using industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all uploaded content
  • Secure data centers with physical security controls
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Download your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Given our lifetime storage promise, photos will be retained permanently unless you request deletion.
